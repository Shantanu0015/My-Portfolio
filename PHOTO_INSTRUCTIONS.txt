To add your photo to the portfolio:

1. Save your photo as "my-photo.jpg" in this same folder (My Portfolio)
2. Make sure the image is:
   - Square or circular crop (recommended: 400x400 pixels or larger)
   - Professional headshot or portrait
   - Good quality and well-lit
   - File size under 2MB for fast loading

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp

If you want to use a different filename, update the src in index.html:
<img src="your-photo-filename.jpg" alt="Shantanu Sarkar">

For best results:
- Use a recent, professional photo
- Ensure good lighting and clear background
- Crop to show face prominently
- Save in JPG format for smaller file size
