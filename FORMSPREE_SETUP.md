# Formspree Setup Guide - Contact Form

## 🚀 **Make Your Contact Form Work in 2 Minutes!**

I've switched your contact form to use **Formspree** - it's free, simple, and works immediately!

## 📋 **Quick Setup (2 minutes):**

### 1. **Create Formspree Account**
- Go to [https://formspree.io/](https://formspree.io/)
- Sign up with your email (free account)
- Verify your email

### 2. **Create a New Form**
- Click "Create a new form"
- Name it "Portfolio Contact" or whatever you like
- **Copy the form endpoint URL** (looks like: `https://formspree.io/f/xxxxxxxx`)

### 3. **Update Your Code**
- Open `index.html` in your portfolio folder
- Find this line (around line 235):
```html
<form id="contactForm" action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID" method="POST">
```

- Replace `YOUR_FORM_ID` with your actual form ID from Formspree
- Example: `https://formspree.io/f/xpznqkka`

### 4. **Set Your Email**
- In your Formspree dashboard, go to form settings
- Add your email: `shanatnu15feb0000@gmail.com`
- This is where you'll receive the messages

## ✅ **Test Your Form**

1. Save the changes
2. Open your portfolio in browser
3. Fill out and submit the contact form
4. Check your email - you should receive the message instantly!

## 🎯 **What You'll Get**

When someone contacts you, you'll receive an email with:
- Sender's name
- Sender's email (you can reply directly)
- Subject
- Message content
- Timestamp

## 🔧 **Features**

- ✅ **Free** (up to 50 emails/month on free plan)
- ✅ **No coding required**
- ✅ **Spam protection**
- ✅ **Mobile-friendly**
- ✅ **Works with your existing design**
- ✅ **Direct email replies**

## 🚨 **Important Notes**

- Formspree free plan allows 50 submissions per month
- Upgrade to paid plan if you get more traffic
- All messages go directly to your email inbox

## 🎉 **You're Done!**

Your contact form now works perfectly! People can send you messages, and you'll receive them in your email. No more "error sending message" issues!
